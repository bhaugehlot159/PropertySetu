Property Care Service Agreement

Monthly care packages, service scope, operational boundaries aur settlement terms ko transparent format me define kiya gaya hai.

Service Scope

Property care plans cover scheduled checks, reporting, cleaning, garden/farm care, bill handling, and tenant coordination as per selected package.

Emergency repairs, consumables, government fees, and third-party vendor costs are billed separately after customer approval.

Visit evidence (photos/checklists/notes) is shared digitally. Missed access due to lock/entry issues may require rescheduling.

Timeline & Termination

Service timelines depend on location, local conditions, and safety restrictions.

Either party may pause/terminate ongoing packages under notice and settlement rules.
