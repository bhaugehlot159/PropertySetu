Refund Policy

Service-wise clear refund framework: featured listings, subscriptions, verification, documentation aur operational packages ke liye transparent rules.

Eligibility Rules

Refund eligibility depends on selected service type (featured listings, verification, subscription, or documentation support).

If a paid service is not activated or fails due to platform error, users may request a refund within the policy window.

Partially consumed services may receive pro-rated or no refunds depending on usage state and operational costs.

Settlement & Verification

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within banking settlement timelines.

Disputed or chargeback-prone accounts may require additional verification before refund decisions.
