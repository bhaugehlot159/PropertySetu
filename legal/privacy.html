Privacy Policy

We collect account, listing, communication, and service-request data only for delivering platform features and support.

Verification documents (such as identity proofs and ownership records) are processed in restricted admin workflows and are not public.

Security logs, device metadata, and activity history may be used for fraud detection, abuse prevention, and performance improvements.

Personal data is not sold to third parties. Data sharing is limited to service operations, legal obligations, or explicit user consent.

Users can request account updates or data deletion subject to legal retention requirements and security checks.
